About the Show

Limit Edge is a groundbreaking fitness competition that pushes athletes to their absolute limits through a series of intense challenges designed to test both physical and mental endurance.

Our Mission

"To inspire strength, endurance, and unity through competition."

What Makes Us Unique

  • Outdoor + indoor challenges
  • Equal male/female competitors
  • Mental + physical endurance tests
  • Real-time audience engagement

Meet Your Host

Reza Madaddi

Fitness Expert & Show Host

Reza Madaddi is a renowned fitness expert, motivational speaker, and the charismatic host of Limit Edge. With over 15 years of experience in the fitness industry, Reza has dedicated his life to helping people push beyond their perceived limitations and discover their true potential.

Challenges

A mix of endurance, speed, and strength challenges.

Endurance

Long-distance runs, obstacle courses, and stamina tests that push athletes to their breaking point.

Speed

Sprints, reaction challenges, and agility tests that demand lightning-fast reflexes and explosive power.

Strength

Weightlifting, power-based tests, and brute force challenges that separate the strong from the strongest.
