About the Show

Limit Edge is a groundbreaking fitness competition that pushes athletes to their absolute limits through a series of intense challenges designed to test both physical and mental endurance.

Our Mission

"To inspire strength, endurance, and unity through competition."

What Makes Us Unique

  • Outdoor + indoor challenges
  • Equal male/female competitors
  • Mental + physical endurance tests
  • Real-time audience engagement
